Welcome to the MoveWithin Demo Site.

The Challenge

When Vanguard first approached us, they had an annual employee turnaround of 18%-20%, which was unusally high for their sector. They were also spending a lot of money on recruiting and attracting new talent to account for this attrition. Their brief to us was simple - "Help control employee turnover & recruitment overheads and establish a framework to reduce attrition."

The Solution

MoveWithin proved to be the perfect solution to Vanguard's problems. The product helped them launch an internal recruitment campaign aimed at employee most at risk of leaving Vanguard. It helped them put their strong culture of entrepreneurship, their proud history and values in focus to retain at risk employees and help them find a career transition within the organization.
